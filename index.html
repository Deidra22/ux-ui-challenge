What is UX and UI?

UX

User Experience

This is a products ability to provide a user with a simple, efficient, and enjoyable experience. Often considered the bones and structure of design.

UI

User Interface

The look and presence of a product. It will vary depending on the target audience. This is known as the appearance and personality of a design.
